<p><p>The electric guitar market is primarily categorized into three types: solid body, semi-hollow body, and hollow body guitars. Solid body guitars are known for their durability and sustain, making them popular in rock and heavy genres. Semi-hollow body guitars combine features of both solid and hollow bodies, offering versatility with a warm tone suitable for blues and jazz. Hollow body guitars, characterized by their fully hollow construction, deliver rich, resonant sounds ideal for jazz and vintage styles. Each type caters to different musical preferences and playing styles.</p></p>

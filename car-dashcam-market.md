<p><ul><li>Single Channel Dashcam</li><li>Multi-Channel Dashcam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car dashcam market is primarily divided into two types: single channel dashcams and multi-channel dashcams. Single channel dashcams record video from one angle, typically facing the road ahead, making them cost-effective and user-friendly. In contrast, multi-channel dashcams feature multiple cameras, allowing simultaneous recording from different angles, such as the front, rear, and sides of the vehicle. This offers enhanced surveillance and safety, catering to consumers seeking comprehensive coverage and increased security against accidents and theft.</p></p>

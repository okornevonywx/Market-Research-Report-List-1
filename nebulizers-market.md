<p><p>The nebulizer market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Pneumatic Nebulizers** utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into aerosol, ideal for various respiratory conditions. </p><p>2. **Ultrasonic Nebulizers** employ high-frequency sound waves to create fine mist, offering a quieter and more efficient delivery. </p><p>3. **Mesh Nebulizers** use a vibrating mesh to produce aerosol, ensuring portable and efficient medication administration with minimal waste. </p><p>4. **Others** may include various specialized devices tailored for specific patient needs or settings, enhancing treatment options.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Microphone</li><li>Decoding Chip</li><li>Electrodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auditory Brainstem Implant (ABI) market comprises three key components: microphones, decoding chips, and electrodes. Microphones capture sound and convert it into electrical signals, enabling auditory perception. Decoding chips process these signals, translating them into a format compatible with neural input for efficient communication with the brainstem. Electrodes are surgically implanted to directly stimulate the auditory pathways. Together, these components facilitate hearing in individuals with auditory nerve damage, driving advancements in ABI technology and expanding market opportunities.</p></p>

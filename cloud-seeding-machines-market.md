Ground-based cloud seeding equipment consists of stationary or mobile systems that release seeding agents into the atmosphere from the earth's surface, targeting specific weather systems. Both methods aim to increase rainfall and mitigate drought conditions, leveraging technology to influence weather patterns effectively.</p></p>

<p><p>The Cupping Devices market comprises various types, primarily classified into traditional cupping and gas tank cupping. Traditional cupping involves suction cups made of glass, silicone, or plastic that create a vacuum effect on the skin for therapeutic purposes. In contrast, gas tank cupping utilizes a machine-driven system that employs gas to create and maintain suction, often providing a more controlled and efficient application. Both types are utilized in alternative therapies to relieve muscle tension and promote circulation.</p></p>

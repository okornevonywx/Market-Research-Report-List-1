<p><strong>The ECG Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ECG Resting System</li><li>ECG Holter Monitoring System</li><li>ECG Stress Testing System</li><li>Cardiopulmonary Stress Testing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG devices market comprises several key types. The ECG resting system records the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest, providing baseline data. Holter monitoring systems offer continuous monitoring over 24-48 hours, capturing heartbeats during daily activities. ECG stress testing systems assess heart performance during physical exertion, often on a treadmill. Cardiopulmonary stress testing systems evaluate both heart and lung function under stress, providing comprehensive insights into cardiovascular and respiratory health for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>

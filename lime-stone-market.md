<p><p>The limestone market is primarily divided into two types: high-calcium limestone and magnesian limestone. High-calcium limestone, primarily composed of calcium carbonate, is widely used in industries such as agriculture, construction, and environmental applications due to its purity and effectiveness in neutralizing acidity. Magnesian limestone, containing a significant amount of magnesium carbonate, is essential in steel production and as a soil amendment. Both types play crucial roles in industrial processes, contributing to sustainable practices and economic growth.</p></p>

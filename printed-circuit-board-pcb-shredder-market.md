<p><ul><li>High-voltage Electrostatic Separation Type</li><li>Non-high voltage Electrostatic Separation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB shredder market is segmented into two primary types based on the electrostatic separation process: high-voltage and non-high voltage electrostatic separation types. High-voltage electrostatic separation types utilize elevated voltage to enhance the separation of metallic and non-metallic components, improving material recovery efficiency. In contrast, non-high voltage electrostatic separation types operate at lower voltage levels, making them suitable for smaller-scale operations or less complex materials, offering a cost-effective solution while still achieving desirable separation results.</p></p>

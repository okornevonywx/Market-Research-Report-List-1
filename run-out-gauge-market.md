<p><strong>The Run-Out Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Run-Out</li><li>Axial Run-Out</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The run-out gauge market consists of two primary types: radial run-out and axial run-out. Radial run-out measures the deviation of a rotating component from its ideal circular path, essential for ensuring balance and alignment in machinery. Axial run-out, on the other hand, assesses the movement of a component along its axis, ensuring proper axial positioning and minimizing wear. Both types are crucial for quality control in manufacturing and maintenance, enhancing operational efficiency and extending equipment lifespan.</p></p>

<p><p>Exoskeleton robots are categorized into three primary market types: lower body, upper body, and full body. Lower body exoskeletons assist with mobility and rehabilitation, primarily enhancing the legs for tasks like walking or climbing stairs. Upper body exoskeletons support arm movements, aiding workers in lifting and manipulating heavy objects, thereby reducing fatigue and injury. Full body exoskeletons provide comprehensive support, integrating both upper and lower body functions, and are often used in rehabilitation, military, and industrial applications.</p></p>

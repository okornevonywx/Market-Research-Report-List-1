<p><ul><li>Dynamic Microphones</li><li>Condenser Microphones</li><li>Ribbon Microphones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overhead microphone market comprises three main types: dynamic, condenser, and ribbon microphones. Dynamic microphones are durable and ideal for live sound applications, delivering reliable performance without the need for external power. Condenser microphones are sensitive and provide superior sound quality, making them preferred for studio recordings. Ribbon microphones offer a warm, natural tone, excelling in capturing vocals and instruments but are more fragile. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to professionals in live events, studios, and broadcasting.</p></p>

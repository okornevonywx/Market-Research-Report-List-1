<p><p>The RFID digital lock market consists of two main types: contact and contactless systems. Contact RFID locks require the user to bring a tag close to the reader, enabling secure access through a physical connection. In contrast, contactless RFID locks allow users to unlock doors from a distance, enhancing convenience and speed. This technology is widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial applications, providing increased security and streamlined access control for various environments.</p></p>

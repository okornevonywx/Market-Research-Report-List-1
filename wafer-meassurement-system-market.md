<p><strong>The Wafer Meassurement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E-beam Meassurement</li><li>Optical Meassurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Measurement System market encompasses various technologies, primarily E-beam and Optical measurement systems. E-beam measurement utilizes focused electron beams to achieve high-resolution surface analysis, making it ideal for advanced semiconductor applications. In contrast, Optical measurement employs light-based techniques, such as reflectometry or interferometry, offering rapid, non-destructive analysis of wafers. Both methods serve critical roles in the semiconductor manufacturing process, ensuring precise control over wafer quality, thickness, and other vital parameters required for optimal device performance.</p></p>

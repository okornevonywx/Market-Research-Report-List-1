<p><p>The carbon monoxide gas sensors market comprises several types, including semiconductor sensors, electrochemical sensors, and biomimetric sensors. Semiconductor sensors utilize metal oxide compounds to detect CO levels by measuring changes in electrical resistance. Electrochemical sensors employ an electrochemical reaction to generate a current proportional to CO concentration, ensuring high sensitivity and accuracy. Biomimetric sensors mimic natural sensing mechanisms, offering innovative detection methods inspired by biological systems. Each type serves distinct applications across residential, industrial, and automotive sectors, enhancing safety and air quality monitoring.</p></p>

<p><p>The trailer refrigeration system market is categorized into single temperature and multi-temperature types. Single temperature systems maintain a uniform climate, ideal for transporting perishable goods that require consistent conditions, such as frozen foods. Conversely, multi-temperature systems offer the flexibility to manage multiple climate zones within a single trailer, enabling the transportation of diverse products simultaneously, like fresh produce and frozen items. This versatility enhances operational efficiency and expands market opportunities for businesses in the logistics sector.</p></p>

<p><p>The Auto Wind Deflectors market features two main types: In-Channel Type and Tape-On Type. Tape-On Deflectors use adhesive to attach to the exterior of the window frame, offering versatility and a more traditional appearance. Both types enhance driver comfort by minimizing wind noise and reducing cabin turbulence while enabling fresh air access.</p></p>

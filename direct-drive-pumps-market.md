<p><p>The direct drive pumps market includes various types, primarily categorized into centrifugal pumps, positive-displacement pumps, and others. Centrifugal pumps operate by converting rotational energy into fluid motion, making them suitable for high-flow applications. Positive-displacement pumps function by trapping a fixed amount of fluid and forcing it through the discharge, providing consistent flow rates, ideal for viscous fluids. Other types may include gear pumps, diaphragm pumps, and peristaltic pumps, each catering to specific industrial requirements and applications.</p></p>

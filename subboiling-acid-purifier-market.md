<p><p>The Subboiling Acid Purifier market primarily features two types of distillation processes: single tube and double tube distillation. Single tube distillation involves a single passage for the liquid, providing a straightforward yet effective separation of acids from impurities. In contrast, double tube distillation uses two concentric tubes, enhancing heat exchange efficiency and achieving purer acid outputs. This design allows for better control over temperature and pressure, resulting in improved purity and reduced operational costs for chemical applications.</p></p>

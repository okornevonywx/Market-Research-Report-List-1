<p><strong>The Chiller Water Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Chiller</li><li>Reciprocating Chiller</li><li>Screw Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chiller water unit market comprises three main types: centrifugal chillers, reciprocating chillers, and screw chillers. Centrifugal chillers use a rotating disk to compress refrigerant, making them suitable for large cooling applications due to their efficiency. Reciprocating chillers utilize pistons to compress refrigerant, offering flexibility for smaller loads. Screw chillers employ twin-screw compressors, providing high efficiency and reliability for mid-sized applications. Each type caters to different capacity and efficiency needs in various commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms</li><li>Ionization Smoke Alarms</li><li>Combination Smoke Alarms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential smoke alarm market features three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric alarms detect smoldering fires using a light sensor, making them effective for slow-burning fires. Ionization alarms use radioactive particles to sense fast-flaming fires but may be less responsive to smoldering fires. Combination smoke alarms integrate both technologies, providing comprehensive fire detection. These diverse options cater to various user needs, enhancing safety in residential environments.</p></p>

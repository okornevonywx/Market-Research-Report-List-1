<p><p>The laser welder market comprises primarily two types: AC laser welders and DC laser welders. AC laser welders utilize alternating current, making them ideal for specific applications involving conductive materials and thicker metals, offering greater heat control. In contrast, DC laser welders operate on direct current, providing a focused and stable beam, which is advantageous for precise welding tasks, especially in thin materials or intricate designs. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing efficiency and quality in welding processes.</p></p>

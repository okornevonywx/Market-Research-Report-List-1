<p><p>The tissue culture roller bottle market features three main types: polystyrene roller bottles, PETG roller bottles, and glass roller bottles. Polystyrene bottles are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for many cell culture applications. PETG bottles offer enhanced clarity and chemical resistance, making them ideal for sensitive cultures. Glass roller bottles provide superior durability and sterilization properties, often preferred for high-value or specialized cell lines. Each type caters to various laboratory needs, ensuring compatibility with different cell culture techniques.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Shunt Active Filter</li><li>Series Active Power Filter</li><li>Hybrid Active Power Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Active Filter market encompasses three main types: Shunt Active Filters, Series Active Filters, and Hybrid Active Filters. Shunt Active Filters mitigate current harmonics by connecting in parallel with loads, improving power quality. Series Active Filters, placed in line with the supply, mainly correct voltage distortions. Hybrid Active Filters combine elements of both shunt and series filters, offering enhanced performance for complex applications. Each type addresses specific power quality issues, catering to varied industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The insecticidal seed treatment market encompasses three primary types: seed dressing, seed coating, and seed pelleting. Seed dressing involves applying insecticides directly to the seed surface, offering protection against pests. Seed coating entails a thicker layer of insecticides, enhancing adherence and protection while allowing for better germination. Seed pelleting involves encasing seeds in a pellet made of inert materials, which provides a controlled release of insecticides, ensuring effective pest management and improving planting efficiency.</p></p>

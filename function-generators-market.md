<p><p>The function generators market is segmented into three primary types: analogue, digital, and sweep function generators. Analogue function generators produce continuous waveforms using analog circuitry, often favored for their simplicity and low distortion. Digital function generators leverage microcontrollers to create precise waveforms and offer more complex signal shapes and modulation options. Sweep function generators are specialized tools that vary frequency over time, useful in testing and measurement applications. Each type caters to diverse needs in electronics testing and research.</p></p>

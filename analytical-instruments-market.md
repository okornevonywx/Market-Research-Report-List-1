<p><p>The analytical instruments market encompasses various techniques used for separating, identifying, and quantifying chemical compounds. Liquid chromatography (LC) separates mixtures in liquid form, commonly used in pharmaceuticals. Gas chromatography (GC) analyzes volatile substances in gaseous phases, frequently employed in environmental testing. Raman spectroscopy utilizes light scatter to identify molecular composition, beneficial in material science. Mass spectrometry (MS) measures mass-to-charge ratios of ions, providing detailed chemical information. The "Others" category includes techniques like FTIR and UV-Vis spectroscopy, catering to diverse analytical needs.</p></p>

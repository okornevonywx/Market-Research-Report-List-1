<p><p>The bar soap market comprises several types catering to diverse consumer needs. Common bar soaps are basic cleansing products, often formulated with fats and oils. Superfatted soaps contain additional oils for extra moisture, appealing to dry skin customers. Transparent soaps use glycerin to create a clear appearance, often marketed as luxury items. Syndet bars are synthetic detergent bars, known for their mildness and pH balance. Combination bars merge properties from both traditional and syndet formulations, targeting versatile use.</p></p>

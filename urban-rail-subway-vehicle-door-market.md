<p><p>The Urban Rail Subway Vehicle Door market primarily consists of two key types: Guest Room Side Doors and Cab Doors. Guest Room Side Doors facilitate passenger access, enhancing convenience and safety within the train, while ensuring efficient boarding and alighting at stations. Cab Doors, on the other hand, provide access to the operator's cabin, ensuring secure entry for conductors and protecting operational controls. Both door types play crucial roles in optimizing the overall functionality and user experience of urban rail systems.</p></p>

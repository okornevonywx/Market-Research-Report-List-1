<p><p>The electric putters market comprises various types, including gear motors, linear actuators, and micro motors. Gear motors provide high torque and efficiency for precise movement, making them suitable for applications requiring significant force. Linear actuators convert rotational motion into linear displacement, ideal for tasks like adjusting angle and position. Micro motors, being compact and lightweight, are perfect for smaller putter designs, offering precision in execution. Together, these components enhance performance, reliability, and user experience in electric putters.</p></p>

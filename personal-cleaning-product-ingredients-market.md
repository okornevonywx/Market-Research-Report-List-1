<p><p>The personal cleaning product ingredients market comprises three main types of surfactants: anionic, non-ionic, and amphoteric cleaners. Anionic cleaners, known for their excellent cleaning power, effectively remove dirt and grease but may irritate skin. Non-ionic cleaners are milder, suitable for sensitive skin, and perform well in hard water conditions. Amphoteric cleaners possess dual properties, allowing them to function as both anionic and cationic, making them versatile for various formulations while being gentle on skin and fabrics.</p></p>

<p><p>The 100mm silicon wafer market is segmented into three main types: Prime Grade, Test Grade, and Monitor/Mechanical Grade. Prime Grade wafers are high-quality, defect-free substrates used for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Test Grade wafers, while still functional, possess more defects and are used for testing processes and devices. Monitor/Mechanical Grade wafers are primarily utilized for equipment calibration and non-critical applications, offering a cost-effective solution for quality control without the need for precision manufacturing standards.</p></p>

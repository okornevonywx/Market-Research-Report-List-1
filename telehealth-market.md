<p><p>The telehealth market comprises three main types: web-based, cloud-based, and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based solutions store data and applications online, ensuring scalability, flexibility, and remote access for users. On-premise systems involve hardware and software installed within a practice's infrastructure, offering greater control over data security but requiring higher maintenance and upfront costs. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>

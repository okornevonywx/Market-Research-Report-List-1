<p><p>The fully electronic smart meters market encompasses two primary types: Direct-Current (DC) meters and Alternating Current (AC) meters. DC meters measure electricity flow in systems powered by direct current, often used in renewable energy applications like solar installations. AC meters, on the other hand, are designed for conventional power grids, monitoring electricity supplied from alternating current sources. Both types enhance energy management through real-time data collection, enabling improved efficiency, accurate billing, and better integration of diverse energy sources.</p></p>

<p><p>The oral dissolving film market includes Oromucosal Film and Orodispersible Film, both designed for rapid drug delivery. Oromucosal Films are intended for administration in the oral cavity, dissolving to release active ingredients directly into the mucous membranes, often used for pain relief or symptom management. In contrast, Orodispersible Films dissolve upon contact with saliva, allowing for convenient and quick intake, ideal for patients with swallowing difficulties. Both types enhance patient compliance and offer efficient dosing solutions.</p></p>
